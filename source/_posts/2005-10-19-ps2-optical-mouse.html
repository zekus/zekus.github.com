--- layout: post title: ps2 optical mouse tags: - Linux status: publish type: post published: true meta: blogger_blog: zekus.blogspot.com blogger_author: zekus blogger_permalink: /2005/10/ps2-optical-mouse.html --- It is sooo strange but this Logitech optical doesnt work on OpenSuse 10.0 and you have to act a trick. I hate this cause people get far from linux when they get problems like this =( and I HATE this... wanna see linux on every machine I sell and I hope this for the future.
Anyway this is the trick:

open Yast -> System -> bootloader configuration
modify the boot option and add psmouse.proto=imps to Other Kernel Parameters
reboot

and that's it!
