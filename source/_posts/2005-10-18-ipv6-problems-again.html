--- layout: post title: ipv6 problems AGAIN tags: - Linux status: publish type: post published: true meta: blogger_blog: zekus.blogspot.com blogger_author: zekus blogger_permalink: /2005/10/ipv6-problems-again.html --- AGAIN since 9.1 version, ipv6 still break your internet connection, delaying DNS answere and giving the TIMEOUT ERROR!

To disable this annoying thing, just edit /etc/modules.conf, go to the line containing "net-pf-10 ipv6" and change to "net-pf-10 off".
Restart your machine and the world will smile again!
