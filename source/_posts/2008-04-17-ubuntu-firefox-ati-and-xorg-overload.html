--- layout: post title: Ubuntu, Firefox, ATI and xorg overload tags: - ati - firefox - Linux - ubuntu - xorg status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- I was stuck with firefox and xorg on my Ubuntu machine: it was so slooooow. Today, finally, I've found a solution: just add the following option to your xorg.conf file under the device section: ``` Option "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps" "true" ``` then reboot your machine or your xorg server only (ctrl-alt-backspace) and ... breath deeply :O
